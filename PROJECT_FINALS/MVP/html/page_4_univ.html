UNIVERSITIES

universities overview
Universities serve as the bedrock of higher learning and personal development, providing a fertile ground for intellectual growth and innovation. Each institution offers unique opportunities through diverse programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and partnerships with leading industries, equipping students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in a competitive global landscape. ,
Adamson University
Adamson University, nestled in the urban landscape of Manila, Philippines, is a prominent private educational institution known for its comprehensive engineering, science, and technology programs.
Ateneo De Manila University
Ateneo de Manila University stands out as a premier educational institution in the Philippines, renowned for its commitment to forming leaders who strive for excellence and serve the greater good.
De La Salle University
De La Salle University (DLSU), located in the heart of Manila, is a prestigious private university recognized for its rigorous academic programs and its strong emphasis on research, community engagement, and innovation.
Far Eastern University
Far Eastern University (FEU) in Manila is one of the Philippines' most respected educational institutions, known for its historic Art Deco campus architecture and a long-standing commitment to providing top-quality education.
Lyceum of the Philippines
Lyceum of the Philippines University (LPU), with its campuses primarily located in Manila and Batangas, offers a diverse and dynamic range of academic programs including international tourism and hospitality management, which is notably distinguished.
Mapúa University
MAPUA University, located in the heart of Manila, is esteemed particularly for its strong emphasis on engineering and technology programs. As one of the leading technical universities in the Philippines, MAPUA incorporates a rigorous curriculum that meets international standards, accredited by several international engineering accreditation bodies
National University
Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), one of the country's largest state universities, offers a vast array of undergraduate and graduate programs at a remarkably affordable cost, making higher education accessible to a broader demographic.
Polytechnic University of the Philippines
National University (NU) in Manila has a rich history of excellence in various fields, notably in health sciences, computer studies, and engineering. With a commitment to providing quality and accessible education, NU has expanded its academic offerings to include programs in business, education, and architecture.
San Beda University
San Beda University, a private Roman Catholic university in Manila, is renowned for its law school, which consistently produces top-notchers in the national bar exams. Beyond law, San Beda offers a wide range of programs including arts, sciences, business administration, and nursing.
University of the East
University of the East (UE), with campuses in Manila and Caloocan, is celebrated for its comprehensive programs in business, dentistry, law, engineering, and arts and sciences. Known for its commitment to innovative and affordable education, UE has garnered recognition both locally and internationally.
University of the Philippines
University of the Philippines (UP), as the country's national university, is a premier institution known for its high academic standards, diverse range of programs, and vibrant campus life. It offers courses in virtually every field imaginable, from engineering and natural sciences to arts and humanities.
University of Santo Tomas
University of Santo Tomas (UST), Asia’s oldest existing university, holds a pivotal place in Philippine education. UST is widely revered for its strong programs in health sciences, including medicine, pharmacy, and nursing, as well as in architecture, engineering, and fine arts.
